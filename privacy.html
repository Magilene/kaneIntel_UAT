Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026. KaneIntel LLC respects your privacy and uses business information responsibly to provide automation consulting and related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect contact information, company details, website form submissions, scheduling preferences, and information you voluntarily provide when requesting consulting, automation, or business support.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, provide assessments, deliver services, improve our website, communicate with clients, and maintain business records.

Automation and Client Systems

When KaneIntel supports automation workflows, CRM systems, reporting, or AI receptionist solutions, we only access data necessary to perform the agreed services. Client data should be handled according to the client’s internal policies, applicable laws, and signed agreements.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers used for email, scheduling, hosting, analytics, CRM, or automation delivery when necessary to operate the business.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No system is completely secure, so clients should avoid sending sensitive medical, financial, or regulated data through public forms unless a secure process has been established.

Your Choices

You may request correction or deletion of contact information by emailing info@kaneintel.com. We may retain certain records as required for business, legal, or compliance purposes.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to info@kaneintel.com.
